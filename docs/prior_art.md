## Motivating findings (PRD §2)

- **OpenAI, NBER w34255 (Sep 2025), "How People Use ChatGPT".** Three topic
  categories (Practical Guidance, Seeking Information, Writing) ≈ 80% of ChatGPT
  conversations; writing dominates work tasks. *To verify: the PRD's claim that this
  paper used WildChat as a validation set — confirm before citing it that way.*
- **Anthropic Economic Index (Jan 2026).** Top 10 work tasks ≈ 24% of Claude.ai
  conversations out of 3,000+ unique tasks. Long-tailed, large head.
- **External-validity note.** Both describe paid/enterprise usage; WildChat is
  consumer free-tier. Concentration here does not transfer cleanly (PRD §2, §12).
